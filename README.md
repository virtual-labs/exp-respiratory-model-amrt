### About the Experiment 

This experiment focuses on the mathematical and physiological modeling of the human respiratory system, providing a platform for students to study the mechanics of breathing, lung volumes, and the dynamic behavior of the respiratory tract under various conditions. Through interactive simulations, users can manipulate parameters such as airway resistance and lung compliance to observe their effects on airflow and pressure, effectively bridging the gap between complex physiological concepts and clinical observation. The lab is particularly valuable for biomedical engineering students as it demonstrates how biological systems can be represented as electrical or mechanical analogues, facilitating a deeper understanding of respiratory monitoring equipment and diagnostic tools without the need for physical subjects.
